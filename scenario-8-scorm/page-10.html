Procedure

Since Velox is a software integration company, the first person Shakib should have consulted was his IDT business partner. They would be able to guide Shakib in the right direction. Shakib should have also consulted the Legal Counsel, since company sensitive information may be shared with Velox, in case any form of NDA needs to be in place.
