Speak Up

It takes courage to Speak Up about wrongdoings. Anyone working for or with the Group who is concerned about actual or suspected wrongdoing at work (whether it is something from the past or likely to happen) should Speak Up.

This applies for employees, contractors, contingent workers, business partners, customers, suppliers, and their workers. If you want to raise your concerns locally, here are the Local Designated Officers.

  • Head of Legal
  • Head of Finance
  • Head of HR
  • Company Secretary & Senior Legal Counsel

Moreover, if we must talk to anyone in the Regulatory area, we should first discuss with the CORA team and check all due diligence before engaging. Engagement with politicians, policy makers and regulators, when carried out transparently and with high regard for accuracy, allows for the best information to be used as a foundation for decision making.
