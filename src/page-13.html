Procurement Policy

The Procurement Policy applies to all indirect goods and services, including Rapide’s engagement. Even though Rapide was a trusted supplier, Shakib should have prioritized renewing their AFC risk assessment before proceeding. Contracts for low- and medium-risk suppliers must be updated every 3 years, while high-risk suppliers require reassessment every 2 years. In this case, Shakib’s actions bypassed the necessary due diligence.

Furthermore, Procurement involvement is mandatory for any external expenditure exceeding BDT 1,000,000 with a single vendor. Shakib should have ensured that Procurement was engaged at the start of the process, followed the company’s sourcing limits, and adhered to using approved suppliers or seeking approval for alternatives when necessary.
