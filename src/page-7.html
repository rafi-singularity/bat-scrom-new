Responsible Marketing Framework

The Group adheres to a set of standards to hold ourselves accountable against our tobacco, nicotine and zero nicotine products and brands. We cannot market our product with images, themes, characters, or hashtags that appeal to the youth.

Using objects such as toys, balloons, and candy is prohibited. The image on the landing page is a strong example of such non-adherence. The fun, vibrant visuals used appeals to the underage crowd and should be avoided. Moreover, 75% of the followers of any chosen brand ambassador of our products should be adults. A pop-culture icon is likely to have a huge teen following.

Moreover, with no age restrictions in place, any accounts tagged in the posts could easily be of somebody underage. Any follower identified as underage should be blocked from following our accounts. Learn more about Responsible Marketing Framework from The Policy Hub!
