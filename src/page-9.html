Anti-Bribery and Corruption

Shakib should not have leveraged his relationship with Tahmid in this manner as Shakib breached a duty of confidentiality. Regardless of the dynamic between the two, offering anything to a Public Official in return for a favour can be considered inappropriate and goes against our SOBC.

The F1 tickets were a blatant example of bribery. The Group mandates that no employee can offer, promise, or give any gift, payment, or other benefit to any person (directly or indirectly) to influence or reward a person of interest.

Moreover, if we must talk to anyone in the Regulatory area, we should first discuss with the CORA team and check all due diligence before engaging. Engagement with politicians, policy makers and regulators, when carried out transparently and with high regard for accuracy, allows for the best information to be used as a foundation for decision making.
