Contract Policy

Shakib broke one of the cardinal rules of the Contract Policy by proceeding with this transaction with Velox without setting up a contract. Since, no existing written agreement or catalogue suppliers were in place, Shakib should have consulted with legal and procurement first. Moreover, he violated one of the clauses of the Contract Policy where the annualised value of the contract exceeds BDT 10mn. All the circumstances which define when a contract is needed can also be found in this policy.
