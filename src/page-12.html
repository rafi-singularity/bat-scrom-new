Procurement Policy

The Procurement Policy applies for all indirect goods and services and Rapide’s intervention was not an exception. Moreover, this policy is dynamic and sourcing limits with a single vendor may be subjected to change at the discretion of the business.

Shakib should have been more diligent with this sourcing, especially considering how Rapide’s contract have already expired. Low or medium risk supplier need to be recertified every 3 years, whereas a high-risk supplier needs to be assessed every 2 years.
