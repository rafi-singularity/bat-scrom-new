A Call for Help

Shakib received a call from Adnan, a regional leaf manager, requesting support. This year’s leaf season was severely impacted by the floods. Adnan mentioned that thirty of the farmers he worked with were displaced, and he wanted to help them get back on their feet. He was seeking a charitable contribution of BDT 10k per farmer from the company. As time was of the essence, he requested Shakib’s support. Shakib suggested to proceed with the disbursement through petty cash and assured Adnan that he would handle it from his end. Shakib goes to his line manager to initiate the process.
