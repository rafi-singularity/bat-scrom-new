Third Party Anti-Financial Crimes Procedure

The AFC Procedure aims to reduce legal and commercial risks posed by third-party relationships. While FastTrack had a strong reputation, their expired risk assessment meant they could have posed potential exposure risks. AFC certification ensures that additional controls and measures are in place to manage any identified red flags.

In Shakib's case, recertifying FastTrack through the AFC process would have allowed the company to evaluate any new risks and implement necessary controls, safeguarding the business from external threats.
